    Tired of waiting on hold to schedule a doctor appointment? DocCaller is an AI-powered appointment scheduling service that makes the call for you. Here's how it works in 3 simple steps:

    1. Enter your information — Fill in the patient details and the name of up to 3 doctors or clinics you want to book with. Don't have the phone number? No problem, we'll look it up.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does DocCaller book doctor appointments for me?

    You enter your patient information and up to 3 doctor or clinic names. Our AI agent then calls each office, speaks with the scheduler, provides your details, and books appointments on your behalf. You receive an email summary when all calls are complete.

    How much does DocCaller cost?

    DocCaller costs $5 per session, which covers up to 3 appointment calls or 60 minutes of total call time. This fee goes directly toward the AI phone agent costs. No subscription required.

    Is my personal information safe with DocCaller?

    Yes. Patient information is not stored and is only used during the scheduling call. We require explicit patient consent before making any calls on your behalf.

    What if the doctor's office doesn't answer?

    If the office doesn't answer, our AI agent will leave a voicemail on your behalf. You'll be notified of the outcome via email along with a transcript of each call.

    Do I need the doctor's phone number?

    No. If you don't have the phone number, just enter the doctor or clinic name and we'll look it up for you automatically.

    Can I schedule appointments at any doctor's office?

    DocCaller works with any doctor's office or clinic that accepts phone-based scheduling. Our AI agent communicates naturally with human schedulers, so it works just like a real person calling on your behalf.

    What types of appointments can DocCaller book?

    DocCaller can schedule any type of medical appointment including primary care visits, specialist consultations, follow-up appointments, annual checkups, dental visits, and more. If you can book it by phone, our AI can handle it.
